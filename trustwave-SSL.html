Sectigo SSL Certificates are digital certificates that provide a secure, encrypted connection between a web server and a browser. They ensure data transmitted over the internet remains private and integral.

Sectigo offers several types of SSL certificates, including Domain Validation (DV), Organization Validation (OV), Extended Validation (EV), Wildcard, and Multi-Domain (SAN) certificates.

  • Organization Validation (OV): Intermediate level, verifying the organization's identity and the domain ownership.
  • Extended Validation (EV): Highest level, providing maximum trust by showing the organization's name in the browser's address bar.

A Wildcard SSL Certificate secures a domain and all its subdomains with a single certificate, making it ideal for websites with multiple subdomain.
